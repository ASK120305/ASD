Biographies

Lionel Messi

Born: June 24, 1987 (Rosario, Argentina)
Position: Forward
Clubs: Barcelona, Paris Saint-Germain, Inter Miami
National Team: Argentina
Lionel Messi is renowned for his dribbling, vision, and playmaking. He spent most of his career at FC Barcelona, winning numerous titles and breaking records. Messi led Argentina to Copa America and World Cup glory.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Born: February 5, 1985 (Funchal, Portugal)
Position: Forward
Clubs: Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, Al Nassr
National Team: Portugal
Cristiano Ronaldo is celebrated for his athleticism, finishing, and leadership. He has excelled in multiple leagues, winning titles and setting goal-scoring records. Ronaldo led Portugal to Euro and Nations League triumphs.
